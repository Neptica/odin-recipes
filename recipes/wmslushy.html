Watermelon Coller Slushy

Description

This is a drink that is sure to cool your day. A great drink for summer.

Ingredients Required

  • 4 cups cubed seedless watermelon.
  • 10 ice cubes.
  • A third cup fresh lime juice.
  • A fourth cup white sugar.
  • An eighth teaspoon salt.

Directions

  1. Put watermelon into a blender.
  2. Add the ice.
  3. Pour in the lime juice, sugar and salt.
  4. Blend until Smooth
  5. Pour and drink!
