Kentucky Tomato Soup Recipe

Description

This creamy tomato soup is delicious and has a twist - the addition of strong, black coffee. This is the perfect summer dish for using freshly picked tomatoes from the garden. Remember - the better the quality of the tomatoes, the better the taste of the soup.

Ingredients Required

  • 3 Tablespoons butter.
  • 1 cup chopped onion.
  • A half cup chopped carrots<./li>
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced.
  • a fourth cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley.
  • 2 and a half cups chopped fresh tomatoes.
  • An 8 ounces of tomato sauce.
  • Three Fourths a cup of strong brewed coffee.
  • A fourth cup of water.
  • 1 teaspoon of white sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon of salt.
  • ground black pepper to taste.
  • A third a cup of heavy cream.

How to Cook

  1. Melt the butter in a pan using medium heat.
  2. Stir the onion, carrots, celery, grarlic, and parsley for about 5 minutes or until the onion has become translucent.
  3. Add the tomatoes, tomato sauce, coffee, water, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring the soup to a boil and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. Pour the soup into a blender and fill it half way full at a time and blend till puree.
